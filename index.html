How tiered memory works

Three storage tiers, automatically maintained. Nothing to think about.

🔥 HOTLast 7 days

Current session context, active tasks, immediate preferences. Loaded first on every session start.

☀️ WARMLast 30 days

Recent decisions and rationale, feedback patterns, project milestones. Cross-referenced during complex tasks.

❄️ COLDForever

Long-term user preferences, historical project decisions, cross-project insights. Consulted for deep context.
